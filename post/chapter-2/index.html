Project I: Parking Space Detection

I am currently working on a parking space detection system for a port in Busan, South Korea. My goal is to design & develop a technology-based solution to optimize the crane’s usage and increase the efficiency of the container unloading process. The system leverages computer vision techniques such as object detection and tracking to monitor the availability of parking spaces near the crane that unloads containers.

The system is designed to automatically detect and track trucks as they approach and leave the parking spaces, and to detect when a space becomes available. This is achieved by using cameras and sensors to monitor the area and feeding the data into an algorithm that processes the images and detects the presence of a truck in a parking space.

Once the system detects an available parking space, it will automatically notify trucks waiting for a space. This eliminates the need for manual monitoring and communication between the crane operator and the truck drivers, which saves time and reduces the potential for errors.

Overall, the parking space detection system is a valuable technological solution that optimizes the container unloading process and enhances the efficiency of the port’s operations.
